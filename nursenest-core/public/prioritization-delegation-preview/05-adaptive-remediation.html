Your pattern: delayed escalation.

NurseNest detected that you recognized deterioration, but chose a lower-priority action before escalation.

Why

Key concept

New confusion plus hypoxia is an acute respiratory safety concern. ABCs and instability override routine medication timing.

Trap

Common mistake

Administering a PRN bronchodilator may be appropriate later, but assessment and escalation come first when breathing is worsening.
