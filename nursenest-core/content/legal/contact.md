# Contact NurseNest

## Support and billing

For subscription help, unrecognized charges, login issues, or product questions, email support@nursenest.ca. We aim to resolve billing questions quickly—contacting support before filing a payment dispute often leads to a faster outcome.

## Privacy requests

For privacy questions or requests (access, correction, deletion where applicable), email support@nursenest.ca with “Privacy” in the subject line.

## Legal and copyright

For legal notices or copyright concerns, contact support@nursenest.ca with “Legal” in the subject line.
