# Contact NurseNest

Need help? Email [support@nursenest.ca](mailto:support@nursenest.ca). Please allow up to 4 business days for a response.

## Support and billing

For subscription help, unrecognized charges, login issues, or product questions, email [support@nursenest.ca](mailto:support@nursenest.ca). Please allow up to 4 business days for a response. For billing, use your [account billing page](/app/account/billing) first when self-serve options are available. Contacting support before filing a payment dispute often leads to a faster outcome.

## Privacy requests

For privacy questions or requests (access, correction, deletion where applicable), email [support@nursenest.ca](mailto:support@nursenest.ca) with “Privacy” in the subject line. Please allow up to 4 business days for a response.

## Legal and copyright

For legal notices or copyright concerns, email [support@nursenest.ca](mailto:support@nursenest.ca) with “Legal” in the subject line.
