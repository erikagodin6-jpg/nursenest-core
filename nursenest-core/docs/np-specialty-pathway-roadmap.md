# NP Specialty Pathway Roadmap

Date: 2026-05-31

Status: internal roadmap. Active pathways may continue using existing public routes. Future pathways remain hidden development targets.

## FNP

Focus:

- Primary Care.
- Family Medicine.
- Women's Health.
- Pediatrics.
- Adult Health.
- Geriatrics.
- Preventive Care.
- Chronic Disease Management.

Readiness domains:

- Health Promotion.
- Primary Care.
- Diagnostics.
- Management.

## AGPCNP

Focus:

- Adult Medicine.
- Geriatrics.
- Complex Chronic Disease.
- Polypharmacy.
- Functional Assessment.
- Long-Term Care.
- Palliative Care.

Targets:

- 300+ specialty lessons.
- 2,000+ specialty questions.

## PMHNP

Focus:

- Mood Disorders.
- Anxiety Disorders.
- Psychotic Disorders.
- Substance Use.
- Therapeutic Communication.
- Psychotherapy.
- Psychopharmacology.
- Crisis Intervention.
- Behavioral Health.

Readiness domains:

- Assessment.
- Diagnosis.
- Treatment.
- Medication Management.

## WHNP

## CNPLE

Focus:

- Canadian NP Competencies.
- Clinical Leadership.
- Health Promotion.
- Population Health.
- Advanced Assessment.
- Diagnostics.
- Prescribing.

Special rule:

CNPLE uses Canadian NP competency and LOFT-style framing. Do not frame CNPLE as CAT, AANP, ANCC, or U.S. FNP.
