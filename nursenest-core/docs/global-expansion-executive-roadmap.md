# Global Expansion Executive Roadmap

Date: 2026-05-31

Status: internal executive roadmap. Hidden international work must remain unpublished until launch approval.

## Recommendation

Do not start by generating thousands of new lessons. Start by recovering, tagging, and reusing the existing global clinical content library. Then build thin country and exam supplements where the actual differences live.

## What Already Exists

- Strong Canada and U.S. pathway architecture.
- NCLEX-RN, NCLEX-PN, REx-PN, CNPLE, NP, Allied, Pre-Nursing, and New Grad content foundations.
- International SEO/blog assets for UK, Australia, India, Philippines, Middle East, and multilingual exam overlays.
- Global clinical content around heart failure, COPD, sepsis, shock, ABGs, ECG, pharmacology, labs, and clinical skills.
- Draft/import/review infrastructure that can hold hidden international inventory safely.

## What Can Be Reused

- Clinical science lessons.
- ECG and telemetry reasoning.
- Lab interpretation reasoning.
- Pharmacology mechanisms and safety.
- Clinical skills procedures.
- Simulation engines and deterioration scenarios.
- Flashcards for universal nursing concepts.
- NCLEX-style clinical judgment scaffolding.

## What Must Be Built

- U.S. legal, healthcare system, Medicare/Medicaid, and delegation supplements.
- UK NMC CBT and OSCE-specific question banks and NHS/NEWS2/Duty of Candour/safeguarding content.
- Australia NMBA/AHPRA/IQNM content plus rural and Aboriginal health supplements.
- New Zealand NCNZ, Te Tiriti, cultural safety, and community care supplements.
- Ireland NMBI-specific content after UK foundation matures.

## What Should Be Delayed

- Public UK/AU/NZ/IE marketing pages.
- Public navigation links for hidden international hubs.
- Separate full country copies of global lessons.
- Large-scale Ireland content generation before UK mapping is complete.
- Screenshot publication for hidden markets.
