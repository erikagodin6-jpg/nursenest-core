## Product Positioning

ECG Certification Academy expands the current ECG ecosystem into a certification-level rhythm interpretation, telemetry, deterioration, and clinical decision-making program. It should build on the existing ECG reasoning library while adding deeper telemetry cases, advanced rhythm analysis, electrophysiology foundations, and emergency response pathways.

## Deterioration Pathway Inventory

| Pathway Family | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Ectopy to instability | PAC to AFib, PVC burden to VT |
| Tachyarrhythmias | SVT to unstable SVT, VT to VF |
| Bradyarrhythmias | Mobitz II to complete heart block, junctional escape deterioration |
| Electrolyte-driven ECG changes | Hyperkalemia to escape rhythm to PEA |
| Ischemia-driven deterioration | STEMI to VT to VF |
| Medication-related rhythms | Digoxin toxicity, QT prolongation, beta blocker toxicity |

## Quality Gates

All advanced ECG content requires review for rhythm accuracy, strip-label consistency, escalation recommendations, medication safety, and scope-appropriate language.

## Global Reuse Strategy

Target reusable global content: 85-90%.

ECG physiology and rhythm interpretation are highly reusable. Country-specific supplements should address role scope, escalation policy, medication naming, and documentation expectations.
