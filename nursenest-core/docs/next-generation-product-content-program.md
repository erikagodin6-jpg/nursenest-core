## Quality Requirements

All future content must:

- Be clinically accurate.
- Be educationally deep.
- Support reasoning.
- Include hints.
- Include clinical pearls.
- Include premium rationales.
- Include related content links.
- Meet content quality score requirements.

Reject:

- Placeholder content.
- Filler.
- Surface-level explanations.
- Question-only assets without rationale.
- Flashcards without clinical value.
- Quantity-over-quality generation.

## Required Content Metadata

Every future asset must include:

- Product.
- Domain.
- Topic.
- Asset type.
- Global/country/exam/institution classification.
- Related lesson IDs.
- Related flashcard IDs.
- Related question IDs.
- Related simulation IDs.
- Readiness domain.
- Competency ID where applicable.
- Quality score.
- Review status.
- Publication flags.

## Readiness Gates Before Any Future Launch

No product can move toward public launch until:

1. Required inventory target is at least 85% complete.
2. Average quality score is at least 85.
3. No placeholder content exists.
4. No duplicate content inflation is detected.
5. Rationale, hint, clinical pearl, and related content fields are complete.
6. Readiness/report-card mapping exists.
7. Subscription gating is validated.
8. Screenshot library is generated from deep learning states.
9. SEO/indexation plan is approved.
10. Clinical review queue is clear for high-risk topics.
