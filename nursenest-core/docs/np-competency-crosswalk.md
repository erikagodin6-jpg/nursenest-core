## Shared Competency Domains

| Domain | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Advanced Assessment | Comprehensive and focused assessment across lifespan, acuity, and specialty contexts. |
| Clinical Decision-Making | Risk stratification, prioritization, differential diagnosis, management, and referral decisions. |
| Diagnostics | Appropriate testing, interpretation, stewardship, and follow-up. |
| Pharmacology | Prescribing safety, mechanisms, contraindications, monitoring, interactions, and education. |
| Health Promotion | Prevention, screening, counseling, immunization, and wellness planning. |
| Leadership | Quality improvement, systems thinking, clinical leadership, and practice improvement. |
| Professional Practice | Ethics, legal scope, documentation, role accountability, and regulatory practice. |
| Evidence-Based Practice | Guideline appraisal, research literacy, and evidence-informed care planning. |
| Population Health | Health equity, epidemiology, community needs, and population-level prevention. |
| Interprofessional Collaboration | Team-based care, referral, consultation, handoff, and shared care planning. |

## Implementation Rules

- Every certification inherits `NP_CORE`.
- Specialty content adds a certification tag only when the learner objective materially differs.
- Readiness scores should weight competencies differently by certification.
- Future pathways must not be public until entitlement, content, clinical, SEO, and navigation gates pass.
