## Market Notes

### United States

Best near-term ROI because the platform already supports U.S. RN, U.S. LPN/PN, and U.S. NP architecture. NCLEX-RN format overlaps heavily with Canadian RN content. Expansion should prioritize U.S.-specific law, delegation, Medicare/Medicaid, documentation, and NCLEX-PN/RN differentiation.

### United Kingdom

Strong English-speaking opportunity with clear differentiators around NMC CBT, OSCE readiness, NHS context, NEWS2, Duty of Candour, safeguarding, and SBAR. Requires regulator-specific review before public launch.

### Australia

Strong value market with NMBA/AHPRA/IQNM alignment, rural care, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander health, and medication regulation needs. Existing Australia SEO/blog assets reduce early market research burden.

### New Zealand

Good fit after Australia because global clinical content and professional standards architecture can be reused. Needs deeper Te Tiriti, cultural safety, NCNZ competency, and community care content before launch.

### Ireland

Promising but should follow UK. Ireland can reuse UK/global clinical content, but NMBI-specific public launch should wait until more direct Irish assets exist.

## Recommended Commercial Sequence

1. U.S. RN/PN expansion.
2. UK RN hidden beta content build.
3. Australia RN hidden beta content build.
4. New Zealand RN hidden beta content build.
5. Ireland feasibility and NMBI content build.
