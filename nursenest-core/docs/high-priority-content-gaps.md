# High Priority Content Gaps

Date: 2026-05-31
Status: Content parity execution planning

Future content flags: `published=false`, `launchReady=false`, `adminOnly=true` until reviewed.

## Ranking Method

Gaps are ranked by learner impact, revenue impact, retention impact, clinical risk, and parity impact. This report uses the count evidence summarized in [content-parity-audit.md](content-parity-audit.md).

## Top Content Gaps

| Rank | Gap | Evidence | Learner Impact | Recommended Action |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | RN question bank below premium threshold | 900 measured in readiness snapshot vs 8,000 threshold | Very high | Add 7,100 RN questions across systems, prioritization, NGN, pharmacology, and high-risk deterioration |
| 2 | RPN/PN question bank below premium threshold | 730 measured vs 4,000 threshold | Very high | Add 3,270 PN/RPN questions with scope-specific rationales and escalation language |
| 3 | Flashcard counts not reliably evidenced by pathway | Prior reports could not score pathway flashcards | Very high | Add a countable flashcard inventory report and then fill gaps to RN 10,000, RPN/PN 5,000, NP 3,000 per certification |
| 4 | Simulations not reliably evidenced at launch depth | Prior audit shows simulation breadth as developing and not scored | Very high | Build scenario inventory and target 250 RN, 150 PN/RPN, and specialty pathway simulation baselines |
| 5 | US NP certifications are thin | US FNP 91 lessons/280 questions; other US NP pathways below 110 lessons and 280 questions | High | Build per-certification NP banks to at least 250 lessons and 2,000 questions each |
| 6 | Allied Health lacks per-profession content parity | Aggregate allied counts do not prove profession readiness; 19 professions failed prior thresholds | High | Start with RT, Paramedicine, MLT, PT/OTA/OT and create 200 lessons/2,000 questions per profession |
| 7 | ECG clinical content below advanced threshold | Prior audit found 43 curated questions vs 300 launch threshold and clinical fidelity gaps | High | Expand ECG questions, deterioration pathways, and publish gates |
| 8 | Pharmacology persistence and analytics incomplete | Prior platform audit scores pharmacology 62% and notes missing persisted class-level content | High | Build medication-class lessons, flashcards, questions, monitoring, and readiness tracking |
| 9 | Admissions content not first-class | No reliable TEAS/HESI/CASPER count evidence in reviewed sources | Medium-high | Build admission exam content after core RN/RPN/NP parity blockers |
| 10 | Professional practice scenarios are underdeveloped | Delegation exists, but documentation, conflict, QI, risk, advocacy are not deep banks | Medium-high | Create dedicated professional practice lessons, questions, and simulations |

## Recommended Order By Learner Impact

1. Complete RN and RPN/PN question depth because these are current core revenue pathways.
2. Instrument flashcard counts, then fill pathway flashcard gaps.
3. Build high-risk clinical loops across RN/RPN/NP because they improve both exam and practice readiness.
4. Expand simulations around the same high-risk topics.
5. Build pharmacology class-level content and medication safety readiness.
6. Bring US NP certifications to minimum standalone depth.
7. Bring allied priority professions to per-profession depth.
8. Build admissions content after core learner products are credible and complete.
