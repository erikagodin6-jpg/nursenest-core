## Product Positioning

CEN Academy should prepare emergency nurses for certification and emergency department readiness. The product should emphasize triage, rapid recognition, stabilization, reassessment, communication, and safe disposition thinking across adult, pediatric, trauma, toxicology, sepsis, cardiac, stroke, and disaster scenarios.

## Lesson Requirements

Every lesson should teach emergency recognition, immediate threats, primary survey priorities, focused assessment, diagnostics, treatment timing, reassessment, disposition considerations, documentation, and clinical pearls.

## Emergency Reasoning Model

All activities should reinforce:

1. Scene or unit safety
2. Primary survey
3. Immediate life threats
4. Stabilization
5. Focused assessment
6. Diagnostics and interventions
7. Reassessment
8. Disposition and handoff

## Global Reuse Strategy

Target reusable global content: 75-85%.

Global emergency concepts can be reused broadly. Country-specific supplements should cover triage terminology, local protocols, medication names, scope, legal reporting, and disaster response frameworks.
