# Future Product Launch Gates

Status: Governance only. Passing a gate does not automatically publish a product.

All stages preserve:

```txt
published = false
launchReady = false
visibleInNavigation = false
indexable = false
adminOnly = true
```

until explicit commercial launch approval.

## Stage 1: Concept

Required evidence:

- Product problem statement
- Target learner and buyer
- Product owner
- Risk class
- Initial content targets
- Reuse hypothesis
- Monetization hypothesis
- Launch locks confirmed

## Stage 2: Content Development

Required evidence:

- Approved curriculum map
- Content coverage targets
- Draft inventory dashboard
- AI content governance workflow
- Duplication checks
- Clinical review plan

## Stage 3: Internal Review

Required evidence:

- Content completeness 60%+
- High-risk clinical review started
- Question/flashcard/simulation QA samples
- Technical feasibility review
- Monetization draft
- Marketing asset plan

## Stage 4: Beta Ready

Required evidence:

- Content completeness 85%+
- Clinical accuracy 90%+
- Technical readiness 90%+
- Analytics events defined
- Beta cohort plan
- Support plan
- Noindex and hidden status verified

## Stage 5: Soft Launch Ready

Required evidence:

- Content completeness 90%+
- Clinical accuracy 95% for high-risk content
- Question quality 90%+
- Monetization path tested, if paid beta
- Marketing beta assets complete
- Institutional pilot reporting ready, if applicable

## Stage 6: Commercial Launch Ready

Required evidence:

- Overall readiness 95%+
- Content completeness 95%+
- Clinical accuracy 95%+
- Monetization readiness 100%
- Technical readiness 95%+
- Marketing readiness 90%+
- SEO readiness 90%
- Operational readiness 90%
- Launch owner approval

## Stage 7: Market Leadership Ready

Required evidence:

- Coverage exceeds launch targets
- Advanced simulations/cases/reporting exist
- Competitive differentiation documented
- Institutional reporting mature
- Outcomes framework established
- Content freshness workflow active
- Revenue, retention, and learner success evidence available

## Blocking Rules

- No public route before Stage 6 approval.
- No navigation before Stage 6 approval.
- No indexing before SEO launch approval.
- No paid launch before monetization readiness is 100%.
- No high-risk clinical content launch without documented clinical review.
