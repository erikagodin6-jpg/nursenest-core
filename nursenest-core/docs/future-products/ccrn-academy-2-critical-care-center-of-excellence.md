| Track | Knowledge Gaps | Clinical Judgment Gaps | Technology Gaps | Ventilator Gaps | Hemodynamic Gaps | Communication Challenges | Medication Safety Risks | Content Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
