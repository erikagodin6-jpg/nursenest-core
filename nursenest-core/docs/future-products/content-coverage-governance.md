# Future Products Content Coverage Governance

Status: Governance only. All incubating products remain hidden, admin-only, unpublished, and noindex.

## Required Target Model

Every future academy must define target counts before content development begins:

- Target lessons
- Target flashcards
- Target questions
- Target NGN cases
- Target simulations
- Target clinical skills
- Target report cards
- Target readiness domains
- Target assessment domains
- Target remediation paths
- Target analytics events

Targets must be broken down by:

- Profession
- Tier
- Body system or specialty
- Difficulty
- Question type
- Clinical risk level
- Monetization tier
- Institutional use case

## Thresholds

| Stage | Required Coverage | Description |
|---|---:|---|
| Minimum Viable Product | 60% | Internal proof of concept. No public exposure. May be used for admin-only review. |
| Soft Launch | 85% | Limited beta candidate. Requires clinical review of all high-risk assets and noindex. |
| Commercial Launch | 95% | Paid product candidate. Requires quality, monetization, technical, marketing, and support readiness. |
| Market Leadership | 110%+ | Competitive depth beyond launch minimums, including advanced cases, simulations, analytics, and institutional reporting. |

## Coverage Rules

- No product may launch with a major domain below 95%.
- No high-risk clinical domain may launch without documented clinical review.
- No question bank may launch if advanced formats are underrepresented.
- No simulation product may launch without debriefing and scoring.
- No institutional product may launch without reporting and assignment evidence.
- No SEO page may index until the product has commercial launch readiness approval.

## Audit Cadence

- Concept stage: monthly
- Content development: biweekly
- Internal review: weekly
- Beta/soft launch: twice weekly
- Commercial launch window: daily blocker review
