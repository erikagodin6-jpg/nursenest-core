# Marketing Readiness Framework

Status: Governance only. Marketing assets do not imply product launch.

## Required Marketing Inventory

Every future product must define and store:

- Feature screenshots
- Mobile screenshots
- Desktop screenshots
- Demo accounts
- Video demonstrations
- Feature comparison assets
- Institutional sales assets
- Social media assets
- Launch announcement assets
- Product one-pager
- FAQ
- Pricing explanation, if monetized
- Support and onboarding copy

## Screenshot Requirements

| Asset | Required Evidence |
|---|---|
| Desktop screenshots | At least primary dashboard, learning activity, analytics/report, settings/paywall state if applicable. |
| Mobile screenshots | Same core flows at mobile viewport. |
| Dark/light or theme variants | Required if product supports themes. |
| Empty/loading/error states | Required for institutional and paid products. |
| Accessibility proof | No clipped text, blocked CTAs, or unreadable contrast. |

## Launch Marketing Checklist

- Product value proposition approved
- Audience and buyer defined
- Feature screenshots complete
- Demo account seeded and stable
- Product video or walkthrough complete
- Comparison positioning reviewed
- Institutional sales deck complete where applicable
- Social launch assets complete
- FAQ and support scripts complete
- Claims reviewed for evidence
- No unsupported superiority, pass-rate, or outcome claims
- Product remains noindex until SEO approval

## Readiness Scoring

- 0-49: No launch story
- 50-74: Internal draft assets
- 75-89: Beta marketing ready
- 90-94: Soft launch ready
- 95-100: Commercial launch ready

No product can launch without marketing inventory sufficient for its sales motion.
