# Clinical Review Framework

Status: Governance only. Clinical review must be documented before launch eligibility.

## Risk Classes

| Risk Class | Examples | Review Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Low Risk | Study skills, general professional development, non-clinical orientation. | Educational review and editorial review. |
| Moderate Risk | Common disease lessons, general assessment, routine documentation, basic clinical skills. | Clinical reviewer plus educational reviewer. |
| High Risk | Critical care, emergency, cardiology, ECG, pharmacology, pediatrics, mental health crisis, NP content, prescribing, medication calculations, invasive procedures. | Credentialed clinical reviewer, second-pass review, evidence/source check, final release owner approval. |

## Specialty Review Requirements

| Area | Required Reviewer Evidence |
|---|---|
| Critical care | Deterioration, hemodynamics, ventilation, escalation, scope boundaries. |
| Emergency | Triage, shock, trauma, time-sensitive pathways, handoff. |
| Cardiology | ACS, dysrhythmias, heart failure, ECG linkage, urgent escalation. |
| ECG | Rhythm interpretation, monitor correlation, intervention safety. |
| Pharmacology | Indications, contraindications, adverse effects, monitoring, teaching, interactions. |
| Pediatrics | Developmental context, weight-based safety, family-centred care, escalation. |
| Mental health | Risk assessment, safety planning, therapeutic communication, crisis pathways. |
| NP content | Diagnosis, differential, prescribing, follow-up, referral. |
| Prescribing content | Jurisdictional scope, contraindications, monitoring, guideline evidence. |
| Medication calculations | Units, rounding, dose range, safety checks. |
| Clinical procedures | Equipment, contraindications, sterile/safety steps, complications. |

## Launch Eligibility

A product is not launch eligible if:

- Any high-risk content lacks reviewer signoff.
- Any reviewer concern remains unresolved.
- Any source is missing for medication, procedure, diagnostic, or prescribing content.
- Any scope-of-practice mismatch exists.
- Any emergency or critical-care scenario lacks escalation guidance.

## Review Evidence

Required per reviewed asset:

- Reviewer name/role/credential
- Specialty area
- Date reviewed
- Risk class
- Decision
- Required revisions
- Final approval status
