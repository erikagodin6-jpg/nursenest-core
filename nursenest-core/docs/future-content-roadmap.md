## Roadmap Principle

Do not add content merely to increase counts. Build content that improves clinical reasoning, exam performance, patient safety, retention, and pathway credibility.

The next content waves should prioritize current ecosystem parity before new product expansion.

## Content Quality Gates

Each new asset must include:

| Asset | Required Quality Elements |
| --- | --- |
| Lesson | Objectives, pathophysiology or concept foundation, assessment/application, safety, clinical judgment, knowledge check, related content |
| Flashcard | Clinically meaningful prompt, concise answer, why it matters, related lesson or topic |
| Question | Hint, correct answer, why correct, why incorrect, clinical application, clinical pearl, exam strategy, related content |
| Case | Patient context, cue set, decision points, outcome, debrief, remediation links |
| Simulation | Branching decisions, patient consequences, scoring, debrief, readiness updates |

Reject placeholder content, filler, superficial explanations, circular rationales, duplicate cards, and unsupported clinical claims.
