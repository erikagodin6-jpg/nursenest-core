# Clinical Reasoning Pathways Engine

## Purpose

The Clinical Reasoning Pathways Engine is the NurseNest flagship clinical judgment training layer. It teaches learners how experienced healthcare professionals reason through patient problems instead of memorizing isolated facts.

## Core Model

Every pathway follows the NCJMM-aligned sequence:

1. Recognize Cues
2. Analyze Cues
3. Prioritize Problems
4. Generate Solutions
5. Take Action
6. Evaluate Outcomes

## Supported Professions

- RN
- RPN/LPN
- NP
- RT
- Paramedic
- OT
- PT
- MLT
- PSW

Each profession track can display different reasoning emphasis while sharing the same patient scenario.

## Pathway Types

- Assessment
- Diagnostic

- Emergency Response

## Teaching Features

The engine supports:

- Expert thinking mode
- Novice thinking comparison
- Reasoning breakpoints
- ABCs, Maslow, acute vs chronic, stable vs unstable, expected vs unexpected, safety risk, and deterioration risk
- Clinical deterioration tracks
- Wrong, partial, delayed, and unsafe decisions
- Consequence feedback
- Thinking error labels such as anchoring bias, confirmation bias, premature closure, tunnel vision, task fixation, and availability bias
- Reasoning score domains for cue recognition, analysis, prioritization, decision-making, and evaluation
