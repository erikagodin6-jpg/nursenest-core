## Mapping Rules

- Clinical science maps to all blueprints unless local standards differ.
- Exam strategy and item format must be exam-specific.
- Scope, delegation, legal, documentation, regulator, and health-system content must be country-specific.
- Readiness domains should inherit from blueprint competencies, not only content categories.
