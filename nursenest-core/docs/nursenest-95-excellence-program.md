Every audited system is below 95%. The largest gaps are concentrated in New Graduate, pharmacology, simulations, Allied, institutional foundations, ECG, NP, study plans, analytics, screenshots, checkout, referral, content governance, content relationships, and mobile/accessibility proof.

Highest-impact gap clusters:

3. Educational quality: ECG fidelity, pharmacology, simulations, rationales, content governance.
4. Retention: readiness parity, study plans, flashcards, report cards, New Graduate.
5. Competitive readiness: CAT, NGN, ECG, labs, pharmacology, simulations, clinical skills, readiness.

## Final Answer

The exact work required to transform NurseNest from 72% completion to 95%+ completion is not expansion. It is proof, reliability, content quality, and completion of existing systems:

1. Make core revenue and access paths provably reliable.
2. Make lessons, questions, flashcards, practice, CAT, and readiness best-in-class.
3. Correct ECG clinical fidelity.
4. Mature pharmacology and simulations.
5. Bring PN/RPN, NP, New Graduate, and Allied to direct evidence parity.
6. Add live product proof to homepage, pricing, hubs, and SEO surfaces.
7. Complete content governance and content relationship coverage.
8. Complete business/institutional reporting and retention analytics.
9. Stabilize testing, CI, and launch dashboards.
10. Keep all international and future expansion work hidden and maintenance-only until existing systems reach excellence.
