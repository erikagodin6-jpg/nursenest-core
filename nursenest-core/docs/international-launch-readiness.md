## Visibility Controls

All hidden international launch candidates must remain:

- Admin only.
- Draft.
- Unpublished.
- Noindex.
- Hidden from navigation.
- Excluded from sitemap generation.
- Not accessible through learner routes.
- Not referenced from homepage, pricing, pathway hubs, or marketing pages.

## Completion Gates

A pathway cannot move from hidden draft to launch review until it has:

1. Blueprint map completed.
2. Lesson inventory completed.
3. Question inventory completed.
4. Flashcard inventory completed.
5. Simulation inventory completed.
6. Readiness domains completed.
7. Country-specific regulator review.
8. Clinical/editorial review.
9. Duplicate and shared-content audit.
10. Paywall and entitlement review.
11. SEO route plan with noindex removed only after approval.
12. Marketing screenshot review with deep learning screens only.
