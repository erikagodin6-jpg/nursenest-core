## Launch Control

No pathway, academy, or initiative advances to commercialization until:

- Current readiness is measured from repository or database evidence.
- Launch blockers are closed.
- Content has clinical review requirements defined.
- Simulation and flashcard maturity are included in readiness scoring.
- No public route, navigation, sitemap, pricing, or learner-facing exposure is created before approval.
