## Program Purpose

This program creates deep, clinically accurate, professionally valuable content inventories for future NurseNest revenue engines before public launch. The work is content foundation planning only. It does not create learner-facing product surfaces, entitlements, routes, pricing, navigation, or SEO exposure.

## Shared Quality Standard

All future content must meet the NurseNest content governance standard before it can move beyond draft inventory:

| Requirement | Standard |
| --- | --- |
| Clinical accuracy | Evidence-aligned, professionally credible, and suitable for clinician review |
| Educational depth | Explains why, how, when, what to do next, and what happens if missed |
| Reasoning model | Reinforces recognize, interpret, prioritize, act, evaluate, and reflect |
| Hints | Guide thinking without revealing the answer |
| Clinical pearls | Memorable, actionable, and practice-relevant |
| Rationales | Include why correct, why incorrect, clinical application, exam strategy, and related content |
| Related links | Connect to lessons, flashcards, questions, simulations, skills, labs, ECG, or readiness domains |
| Quality score | Target 85 or higher before publish consideration |
| Duplication control | Prefer global reusable content plus country or exam supplements |

Reject content that is placeholder, filler, superficial, repetitive, factually weak, hallucinated, or generated only to increase counts.

## Hidden Development Gates

Before any future product can be considered for launch planning, it must satisfy:

| Gate | Required Evidence |
| --- | --- |
| Content inventory | Target lesson, flashcard, question, case, and simulation scope documented |
| Quality governance | Required content quality rubric attached |
| Reuse strategy | Global, country-specific, and exam-specific split identified |
| Clinical review plan | High-risk content review categories defined |
| Product readiness model | Readiness domains and report-card signals outlined |
| Entitlement plan | Subscription and institutional access model drafted |
| Public safety | No navigation, sitemap, indexation, or unauthenticated launch surface |
