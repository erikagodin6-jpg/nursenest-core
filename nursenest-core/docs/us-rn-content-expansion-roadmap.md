## Draft Pipeline Rules

All new generated content starts as:

- `status=draft`
- `published=false`
- `visibleInNavigation=false`
- `launchReady=false`

Promotion requires:

1. Clinical accuracy review.
2. NCLEX-RN blueprint alignment.
3. Rationale quality review.
4. Duplicate-content scan.
5. Country-specific scope check.
6. Accessibility and rendering check.
