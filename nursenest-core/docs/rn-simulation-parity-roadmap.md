## RN Build Sequence

1. Fill high-risk gaps first: GI bleed, maternal emergencies, pediatric emergencies, ACS, stroke, DKA, hyperkalemia.
2. Add RN pathway mapping to every simulation before counting toward NCLEX-RN readiness.
3. Add debrief and readiness signals to every simulation.
4. Tie simulation misses to flashcards, questions, lessons, and NGN cases.
5. Keep future CCRN/CEN positioning hidden until RN parity exceeds 95%.
