## Build Sequence

1. Complete the 3,270-question PN/RPN gap with scope-safe rationales.
2. Prioritize escalation, reporting, documentation, delegation, and medication safety because these prevent RN-centric PN content.
3. Build REx-PN and NCLEX-PN flashcard inventories separately; do not assume Canada and US PN cards are interchangeable.
4. Create 142 additional RPN simulation references, starting with predictable-to-unstable deterioration, medication safety, falls, infection, COPD, sepsis, and hypoglycemia.
5. Add launch signals that explicitly confirm PN scope boundaries and escalation expectations.
