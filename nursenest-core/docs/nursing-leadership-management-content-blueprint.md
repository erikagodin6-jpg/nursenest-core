## Product Positioning

Nursing Leadership & Management should prepare learners and practicing nurses for delegation, staffing, conflict resolution, quality improvement, risk management, accountability, healthcare policy, and leadership judgment. It should support exam preparation, new graduate growth, charge nurse readiness, and future institutional leadership programs.

## Scenario Standards

Leadership scenarios should require the learner to:

1. Identify the leadership problem
2. Prioritize safety and fairness
3. Choose communication language
4. Apply scope and policy
5. Document appropriately when needed
6. Evaluate team and patient outcomes

## Global Reuse Strategy

Target reusable global content: 70-80%.

Leadership principles are reusable. Country-specific supplements should address professional standards, legal reporting, delegation rules, collective agreements, healthcare policy, and workplace terminology.
