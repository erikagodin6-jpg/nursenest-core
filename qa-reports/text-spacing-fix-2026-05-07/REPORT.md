# Inline text spacing — auth / signup / login / legal / footer

**Date:** 2026-05-07  
**Scope:** Collapsed or missing word boundaries in marketing auth legal lines and related i18n.

## 1. Root causes (categorized)

| Category | What went wrong | Example |
|----------|------------------|---------|
| **JSX whitespace** | Newline-only gap between `{t("…legalBefore")}` and `<Link>` is not a reliable space once `legalBefore` strings were normalized without a trailing space. | User-visible `ourTerms of Service` style joins. |
| **i18n interpolation / literals** | `pages.signup.legalAnd` missing surrounding spaces in some locales (`es`: `y`, `tl`: `at`) glued link labels to the conjunction. | `Terms of ServiceandPrivacy Policy` style joins. |
| **Locale-specific `legalBefore`** | Chinese login string ended in `我们的` with no trailing space before English policy link labels; Japanese login ended with full-width colon with no following space. | `我们的Terms…`, `：Terms…` risk. |

No flex-on-`<p>` stripping was required for the auth legal `<p>` (it is not a flex container). Checkout policy checkbox copy already uses comma-separated keys with explicit spaces.
