B High-Fowler's + O₂
Correct Answer
Clinical Rationale

In acute HF exacerbation with hypoxia, positioning and oxygenation are immediate priorities. High-Fowler's reduces venous return and relieves dyspnea, while supplemental O₂ addresses the SpO₂ of 92%.

Why not A? Furosemide, while appropriate, is a subsequent intervention after stabilizing oxygenation.

NCLEX Strategy
Use the ABCs and Maslow's: Airway → Breathing → Circulation. Airway management (positioning + O₂) precedes pharmacological intervention.
