## SEO Rules For French Canadian Blogs

Every blog article must include:

- `locale: fr-CA`,
- Canadian title and description,
- explicit exam or profession target,
- French Canadian FAQ section,
- breadcrumb hierarchy,
- related lessons/questions/flashcards previews,
- clinical review metadata,
- language review metadata,
- references where clinical claims are made,
- `indexable: false` until the full pathway or hub gate is approved.
