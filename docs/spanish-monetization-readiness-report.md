Date: 2026-05-31

## Executive Summary

Spanish monetization is not ready. Spanish is a major growth opportunity, but selling a Spanish pathway before the learner experience is complete would damage trust. The current posture should be audience-building, waitlist capture, and free Spanish mini-packs after clinical/language review.

## Paid Launch Minimum

Do not launch paid Spanish NCLEX-RN until at least:

- 150 Spanish lessons,
- 2,000 Spanish questions,
- 3,000 Spanish flashcards,
- 30 NGN case studies,
- 40 bowties,
- 40 matrix cases,
- Spanish onboarding,
- Spanish pricing and checkout copy,
- Spanish email lifecycle,
- Spanish support expectations,
- reviewed premium previews.

## Trust Guardrails

Spanish subscription pages must clearly disclose:

- what is fully available in Spanish,
- what remains English-only if any,
- whether support is available in Spanish,
- exam and regulatory disclaimers,
- clinical review status,
- no guarantee of passing or licensure.
