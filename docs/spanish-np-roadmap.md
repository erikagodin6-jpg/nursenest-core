## Spanish NP Language Requirements

Spanish NP content requires advanced clinical language. It must handle:

- diagnostico diferencial,
- signos de alarma,
- manejo farmacologico,
- prescripcion segura,
- interacciones medicamentosas,
- monitoreo renal/hepatico,
- salud mental and psychiatric terminology,
- documentacion clinica,
- consentimiento informado,
- derivacion and consultation.

## Recommendation

Start Spanish NP with FNP only. AGPCNP and PMHNP should remain planning queues until:

- Spanish FNP terminology is validated,
- NP reviewer capacity is secured,
- Spanish advanced pharmacology review is reliable,
- certification-specific blueprints are mapped.
