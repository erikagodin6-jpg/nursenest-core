Date: 2026-05-31

## Assumptions

This estimate assumes original Spanish clinical authoring or deep clinical adaptation, not literal translation. It includes authoring, clinical review, Spanish language review, SEO review, metadata, and QA.

## Staffing Recommendation

Minimum Spanish ecosystem team:

- 1 Spanish nursing content director,
- 3-6 Spanish nursing writers,
- 1 NCLEX-RN reviewer,
- 1 NCLEX-PN reviewer,
- 1 FNP reviewer,
- 1 psychiatric NP reviewer before PMHNP production,
- 1 AGPCNP reviewer before AGPCNP production,
- 1 Spanish medical language editor,
- 1 international SEO lead,
- 1 content QA/governance analyst.

## Fastest Safe Path

1. Build Spanish NCLEX-RN pilot.
2. Validate Spanish clinical language and rationale quality.
3. Build Spanish SEO/waitlist funnel.
4. Expand NCLEX-RN to paid pilot threshold.
5. Start NCLEX-PN after RN pipeline stabilizes.
6. Start FNP as advanced-practice pilot.
7. Defer AGPCNP and PMHNP until reviewer capacity is secured.
