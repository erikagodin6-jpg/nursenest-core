## Free vs Premium Model

Future French public pages may include:

- exam overview pages,
- career and licensing guides,
- selected clinical education pages,
- sample care plan or lesson excerpts,
- selected free questions,
- downloadable checklists.

Premium should remain protected:

- full question banks,
- CAT/practice exams,
- NGN case studies,
- bowties and matrix sets,
- adaptive study plans,
- readiness analytics,
- simulations,
- flashcard mastery loops,
- clinical reasoning pathways,
- detailed performance reports.

## French Conversion Requirements

Every French public page should include:

- primary CTA in French,
- secondary CTA in French,
- clear premium preview card,
- account creation CTA,
- subscription/trial CTA only after value is established,
- trust signals with clinical review status,
- French FAQ,
- related French assets.

## Subscription Guardrails

Do not charge for a French pathway unless:

- product UI is fully usable in French,
- critical educational assets are in French,
- support expectations are clear,
- premium previews accurately represent the paid experience,
- English-only fallbacks are disclosed.

## Monetization Recommendation

Start with French REx-PN waitlist/account capture and free mini-packs, not immediate paid subscriptions. Move to paid French REx-PN only after the first clinically reviewed premium loop exists:

- 100 lessons,
- 1,000 questions,
- 1,500 flashcards,
- 20 case studies,
- 25 bowties,
- 25 matrix questions,
- onboarding and study plan labels in French,
- pricing and emails in French.
