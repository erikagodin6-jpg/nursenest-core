## Staffing Recommendation

Minimum viable content team:

- 1 French Canadian nursing content lead,
- 2-4 French Canadian nursing writers,
- 1 practical nursing reviewer,
- 1 RN reviewer,
- 1 NP reviewer,
- 1 French Canadian medical language editor,
- 1 SEO/content operations lead,
- 1 QA/content governance analyst.

## Fastest Safe Path

1. Build REx-PN French first.
2. Commission 50 flagship REx-PN lessons, 500 questions, 1,000 flashcards, and 50 SEO articles.
3. Validate tone, terminology, workflow, and review quality.
4. Expand to the remaining REx-PN inventory.
5. Reuse the production workflow for Canadian NCLEX-RN.
6. Start CNPLE after reviewer capacity is confirmed.
