## Executive Summary

NurseNest is a comprehensive nursing education platform serving RPN/LVN, RN/NCLEX, and NP students in Canada and the US. The platform already has strong SEO foundations including dynamic meta tags, OG tags, JSON-LD structured data, GA4/GTM integration, sitemap.xml, robots.txt, hreflang tags, and programmatic SEO pages. This strategy document outlines the next phase of growth: content-driven organic traffic acquisition, featured snippet targeting, programmatic page expansion, and backlink building.

**Target Audience:**
- Pre-nursing students researching programs
- RPN/LVN students preparing for REx-PN (Canada)
- RN students preparing for NCLEX-RN (US/Canada)
- NP students preparing for AANP, ANCC, and specialty certification exams
- International nursing graduates (IENs) preparing for licensure

**Primary Revenue Model:** Subscription tiers (RPN $29.99/mo, RN $39.99/mo, NP $49.99/mo)

## FAQ Sections for Featured Snippets

### NCLEX FAQs (Target Position Zero)
- **"How many questions is the NCLEX-RN?"** The NCLEX-RN uses computer adaptive testing with 75-145 questions. As of 2023, the minimum is 85 questions.
- **"What is the NCLEX-RN passing score?"** The NCLEX uses a pass/fail system based on the logit passing standard, currently set at 0.00 logits above the passing standard.
- **"How long does it take to get NCLEX results?"** Quick Results are available 48 hours after testing for $7.95. Official results come from your state board within 2-6 weeks.
- **"Can you use a calculator on the NCLEX?"** Yes, the NCLEX provides an on-screen calculator for dosage calculation questions.
- **"How many times can you retake the NCLEX?"** You can retake the NCLEX after a 45-day waiting period. Most states allow unlimited attempts.

### REx-PN FAQs (Target Position Zero)
- **"What is the REx-PN exam?"** The Regulatory Exam for Practical Nurses (REx-PN) is the Canadian licensure exam for practical nursing graduates, replacing the CPNRE in January 2022.
- **"How many questions is the REx-PN?"** The REx-PN has approximately 150-170 questions using computer adaptive testing (CAT).
- **"Is the REx-PN harder than the CPNRE?"** The REx-PN uses adaptive testing technology which adjusts question difficulty based on your performance, making it different from the fixed-form CPNRE.

### NP Certification FAQs
- **"How long is the AANP exam?"** The AANP FNP exam is 3 hours with 150 questions (135 scored, 15 pretest).
- **"What is the AANP pass rate?"** The national first-time pass rate for the AANP FNP exam is approximately 85%.
- **"Do nurse practitioners need a DEA number?"** NPs who prescribe controlled substances need a DEA registration in addition to their state prescriptive authority.
