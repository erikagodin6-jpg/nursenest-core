## Purpose

Create a future Spanish-language SEO and localization structure serving US Hispanic nurses, Puerto Rico, Mexico, Central America, South America, and internationally educated nurses preparing for NCLEX.

## Country Page Requirements

Every country should eventually have:

- country-specific NCLEX landing page,
- country-specific FAQ,
- licensing/credentialing overview,
- salary/career guide where reliable sources exist,
- study guide,
- international nurse pathway guide,
- local terminology notes,
- related Spanish NCLEX resources,
- disclaimers for regulatory and immigration information.

## Localization Notes

Do not assume one Spanish variant fits all contexts. Keep the first product as professional international Spanish, then add country-specific notes for:

- licensure vocabulary,
- educational credential terminology,
- nursing role names,
- medication brand/name differences,
- documentation language,
- immigration/regulatory disclaimers,
- salary/currency context,
- local search phrasing.

## Country-Specific Governance

Country pages must be reviewed for:

- regulatory accuracy,
- claims about eligibility,
- visa/immigration disclaimers,
- source quality,
- date freshness,
- no unsupported salary claims,
- no implication that NurseNest grants licensure or immigration approval.
