## Fastest Path to Manual-Review Readiness

1. Make existing governance visible.
2. Add named humans where the platform currently uses institutional/team labels.
3. Add references where clinical claims appear.
4. Publish outcome methodology before outcome claims.
5. Use healthcare authority pages as the template for every public YMYL surface.
6. Remediate the lowest E-E-A-T pages and internal-link gaps already identified in audit artifacts.

## Roadmap Principle

NurseNest does not need louder marketing to become more authoritative. It needs visible, structured proof that the educational ecosystem is authored, reviewed, sourced, updated, and honest about what it can and cannot claim.
