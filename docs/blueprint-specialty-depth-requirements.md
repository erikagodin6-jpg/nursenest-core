# Blueprint Coverage And Specialty Depth Requirements

Date: 2026-05-31

Purpose: prevent exam pathways from being marked complete based on raw inventory counts alone.

Meeting total question, flashcard, lesson, practice exam, CAT, or simulation counts is not sufficient. Every pathway must demonstrate adequate depth across blueprint domains, body systems, specialties, patient populations, and nursing competencies.

No pathway may be marked complete if major specialty gaps exist.

This standard complements:

- `docs/content-inventory-maturity-standard.md`
- `docs/international-expansion-roadmap.md`
- `docs/global-nursing-architecture-masterplan.md`

## Specialty Distribution Requirements

Each exam pathway must meet these minimum proportions unless an official exam blueprint requires a stricter or materially different distribution.

| Domain | Minimum share of total question inventory |
|---|---:|
| Adult medical-surgical nursing | 25% |
| Pharmacology | 15% |
| Pediatric nursing | 10% |
| Maternal-newborn nursing | 10% |
| Mental health nursing | 10% |
| Critical care and emergency nursing | 8% |
| Leadership and management | 5% |
| Community and public health | 5% |
| Professional practice and ethics | 5% |

These minimums intentionally do not sum to 100%. Remaining inventory should be allocated based on the exam blueprint, pathway type, specialty emphasis, clinical judgment needs, and country-specific regulatory requirements.

## Adult Medical-Surgical Nursing

Minimum: 25% of total question inventory.

Must include:

- cardiovascular,
- respiratory,
- neurological,
- gastrointestinal,
- endocrine,
- renal,
- musculoskeletal,
- hematology,
- oncology,
- infectious disease,
- dermatology,
- immunology,
- palliative care.

## Pharmacology

Minimum: 15% of total question inventory.

Must include:

- cardiovascular drugs,
- endocrine drugs,
- respiratory drugs,
- psychiatric drugs,
- antimicrobials,
- oncology medications,
- pain management,
- emergency medications,

## Laboratory Interpretation

Every pathway must contain at least 500 laboratory-focused questions.

Must include:

- CBC,
- electrolytes,
- renal labs,
- liver labs,
- coagulation studies,
- ABGs,
- cardiac markers,
- endocrine testing,
- therapeutic drug monitoring.

## ECG And Cardiac Monitoring

Every RN and NP pathway must contain at least 500 ECG-focused questions.

Must include:

- rhythm identification,
- dysrhythmia management,
- STEMI recognition,
- conduction abnormalities,
- pacemakers,
- emergency interventions.

PN/RPN, allied health, and international pathways should include ECG inventory where the pathway blueprint, role scope, and clinical expectations require it.

## Clinical Skills

Every pathway must contain at least 250 skills-focused scenarios.

Examples:

- medication administration,
- IV therapy,
- central lines,
- wound care,
- tracheostomy care,
- chest tubes,
- blood transfusions,
- isolation precautions.

## Simulation Requirements

Every pathway must contain at least 50 full simulations.

Simulation categories:

- medical-surgical,
- emergency,
- critical care,
- pediatric,
- maternal-newborn,
- mental health,
- community health.

Every simulation must include:

- patient profile,
- assessment findings,
- clinical deterioration logic,
- documentation tasks,
- communication tasks,
- handoff tasks,
- debriefing.

## Gap Analysis Requirement

After every content generation cycle, audit:

- question counts,
- lesson counts,
- flashcard counts,
- specialty coverage,
- blueprint coverage,
- clinical judgment coverage,
- simulation coverage.

Identify:

- weakest specialty,
- weakest body system,
- weakest question format,
- weakest lesson category.

The next content generation cycle must prioritize the weakest existing gaps.

No new specialty expansion should occur until all existing specialties achieve at least 95% completion.

## Completion Gate

A pathway cannot be marked complete unless:

- total inventory targets are met,
- specialty distribution minimums are met,
- required subtopics are represented,
- lab and ECG requirements are met where applicable,
- clinical skills scenarios meet the minimum threshold,
- simulation requirements are met,
- blueprint coverage is at least 95%,
- specialty coverage is at least 95%,
- clinical judgment coverage is at least 95%,
- content quality and publication metadata gates pass.

If any major specialty, body system, patient population, or required competency is below threshold, the pathway remains:

```text
DRAFT - NOT READY FOR PUBLICATION
```

Only pathways that satisfy inventory, quality, blueprint, specialty depth, localization where applicable, and adaptive learning requirements may be marked:

```text
READY FOR PUBLICATION
```
