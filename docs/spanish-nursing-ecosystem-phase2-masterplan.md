## Purpose

Build the planning foundation for a Spanish-language NurseNest nursing ecosystem serving:

- United States Hispanic nurses,
- Puerto Rico,
- Mexico,
- Central America,
- South America,
- internationally educated nurses preparing for NCLEX,

## Key Finding

Spanish infrastructure exists, but Spanish pathway publication readiness is 0%. The existing 140 `es-intl` drafts are generic international Spanish resources. They are not sufficient for Spanish NCLEX-RN, NCLEX-PN, FNP, AGPCNP, or PMHNP launch.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not machine-translate English content.
- Do not publish Spanish pages until review gates pass.
- Do not include Spanish pages in sitemap or hreflang while preview/noindex.
- Do not claim Spanish premium readiness before the learner product is usable in Spanish.
- Do not use awkward literal healthcare translations.
- Do not flatten Spanish for every region; use professional international Spanish with country-specific guidance where needed.
