## Build Strategy

NCLEX-PN Spanish should not be a reduced copy of RN content. It needs PN/LPN scope-specific framing:

- data collection and reporting,
- safe medication administration within scope,
- delegation/assignment boundaries,
- stable vs unstable patient recognition,
- long-term care and medical-surgical realities,
- patient teaching and documentation,
- escalation to RN/provider when patient status changes.
