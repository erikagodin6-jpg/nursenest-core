# Delegation Questions for NCLEX

Delegation questions are extremely common on the NCLEX-RN because they evaluate safe nursing judgment and scope-of-practice understanding.

Students must understand:
- RN responsibilities,
- LPN/RPN delegation,
- UAP task assignment,
- and patient stability.

The NCLEX often tests:
- unstable versus stable patients,
- assessment responsibilities,
- and tasks requiring clinical judgment.

Strong delegation skills are closely tied to prioritization and patient safety.
