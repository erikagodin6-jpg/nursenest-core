# Pharmacology Questions for NCLEX

Pharmacology questions are among the highest-yield topics on the NCLEX-RN.

Students are commonly tested on:
- medication safety,
- side effects,
- contraindications,
- administration priorities,
- and patient teaching.

The NCLEX focuses heavily on safe medication administration and clinical judgment.

Strong pharmacology preparation should include:
- repeated question practice,
- high-yield medication patterns,
- and rationale review.
