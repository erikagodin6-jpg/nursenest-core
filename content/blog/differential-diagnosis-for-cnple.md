# Differential Diagnosis for CNPLE

Differential diagnosis is a major focus of the CNPLE because nurse practitioners must evaluate complex patient presentations safely.

Students should practice:
- identifying red flags,
- comparing diagnoses,
- prioritizing dangerous conditions,
- and selecting appropriate investigations.

The exam frequently tests:
- evolving symptoms,
- chronic disease presentations,
- and evidence-based management.

Strong preparation should include repeated case-based clinical reasoning practice.
