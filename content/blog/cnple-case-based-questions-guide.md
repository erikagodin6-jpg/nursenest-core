# CNPLE Case-Based Questions Guide

Case-based questions are a major component of NP-level clinical judgment exams such as the CNPLE.

These questions evaluate:
- differential diagnosis,
- management decisions,
- prescribing safety,
- and clinical reasoning.

Students preparing for the CNPLE should practice:
- patient scenarios,
- evolving cases,
- and prioritization-based decision-making.

Strong performance requires:
- clinical application,
- not just memorization.
