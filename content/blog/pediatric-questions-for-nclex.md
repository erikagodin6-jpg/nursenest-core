# Pediatric Questions for NCLEX

Pediatric nursing questions on the NCLEX evaluate:
- developmental milestones,
- family-centered care,
- medication safety,
- growth and development,
- and pediatric emergencies.

Students commonly struggle with:
- age-specific findings,
- communication strategies,
- and pediatric prioritization.

The best preparation includes:
- pediatric case scenarios,
- adaptive question banks,
- and repeated rationale review.
