# Best Way to Study for REx-PN

Preparing for the REx-PN requires more than memorizing textbook content.

Successful students usually focus on:
- adaptive question practice,
- prioritization,
- clinical judgment,
- and rationale review.

The most effective study methods include:
- daily question exposure,
- weak-area tracking,
- and repeated practice with CAT-style exams.

Students who combine active learning with structured review are often more successful on the REx-PN.
