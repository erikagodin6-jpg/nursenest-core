# Maternity Questions for NCLEX

Maternity questions on the NCLEX commonly test:
- fetal monitoring,
- labor stages,
- postpartum complications,
- obstetric emergencies,
- and newborn assessment.

Students often struggle with:
- prioritization during labor,
- interpreting fetal heart tracings,
- and postpartum safety.

Strong maternity preparation should combine:
- clinical scenarios,
- SATA questions,
- and prioritization practice.
