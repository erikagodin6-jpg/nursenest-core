# CNPLE Pharmacology Study Guide

Pharmacology is one of the most challenging sections of the CNPLE because the exam emphasizes prescribing safety and clinical decision-making.

Students should focus on:
- primary care medications,
- contraindications,
- monitoring,
- adverse effects,
- and patient education.

The CNPLE often tests:
- safe prescribing,
- chronic disease management,
- and evidence-based pharmacologic decisions.

Case-based pharmacology practice is one of the best preparation methods.
