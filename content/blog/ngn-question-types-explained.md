# NGN Question Types Explained

The Next Generation NCLEX introduced new clinical judgment question formats designed to evaluate how nursing students think through patient care decisions.

These question types include:
- bowtie questions
- case studies
- trend questions
- matrix multiple response
- extended SATA
- and drag-and-drop prioritization.

Unlike traditional NCLEX questions, NGN formats focus heavily on:
- recognizing cues,
- analyzing findings,
- prioritizing hypotheses,
- and evaluating outcomes.

Students preparing for NGN exams should focus on:
- clinical reasoning,
- repeated question exposure,
- and rationale review.
